# Company Information

Configure your company details that appear on documents and communications.

[image_company_settings_page]

## Accessing Company Settings

1. Navigate to **Admin > Settings**
2. Click on **Company** tab

## Company Details

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Company Name** | Legal business name |
| **Address** | Business address |
| **Phone** | Contact phone number |
| **Email** | Contact email address |
| **Website** | Company website URL |
| **Tax Number** | Tax registration number |
| **Registration Number** | Business registration number |


### Company Name

Your official business name. This appears on:
- Invoices and receipts
- Quotations
- Warranty certificates
- Email communications

### Address

Your business address. Displayed on:
- Invoice headers
- Quotation headers
- Contact information

### Contact Information

- **Phone** - Primary contact number
- **Email** - Business email for customer inquiries
- **Website** - Your company website URL

### Legal Numbers

- **Tax Number** - Your tax registration/VAT number
- **Registration Number** - Business registration number

These appear on official documents for legal compliance.

## Company Branding

### Company Logo

Upload your company logo for documents:
- Appears on invoices, quotations, receipts
- Recommended size: 200x80 pixels
- Format: PNG with transparent background


### Company Stamp

Upload your company stamp/seal (optional):
- Used on official documents
- Recommended size: 150x150 pixels
- Format: PNG with transparent background

## Warranty Settings

[image_warranty_settings]

Configure default warranty information for repair orders.

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Default Warranty Period** | Default months of warranty (0-120) |
| **Warranty Policy** | Your warranty policy text |
| **Warranty Terms** | Detailed warranty terms and conditions |


### Default Warranty Period

Set the default number of months for warranty. This is automatically applied to new repair orders but can be adjusted per order.

### Warranty Policy

Brief warranty policy statement. Displayed on:
- Repair order details
- Warranty certificates

Example:
> We provide warranty for parts and labor. Warranty does not cover physical damage, water damage, or unauthorized modifications.

### Warranty Terms

Detailed terms and conditions. Include:
- What is covered
- What is not covered
- How to claim warranty
- Contact information for warranty claims

## Saving Changes

1. Fill in all required fields
2. Upload logo and stamp if needed
3. Click **Save Settings**
