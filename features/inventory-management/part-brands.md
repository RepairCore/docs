# Part Brands

Manage part manufacturers and brands.

[image_part_brands_list]

## Overview

Part brands help identify manufacturers of your parts. Assign brands to parts for better organization and filtering.

## Viewing Brands

Navigate to **Admin > Inventory > Part Brands** to see all brands.

[image_part_brands_index]

## Creating a Brand

1. Navigate to **Admin > Inventory > Part Brands**
2. Fill in the form on the left side
3. Click **Save**

[image_create_part_brand]

### Brand Fields

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Name** | Brand name | Yes |
| **Logo** | Brand logo image | No |
| **Website** | Brand website URL | No |
| **Description** | Brand description | No |
| **Status** | Active or Inactive | Yes |

## Editing a Brand

1. Find the brand in the list
2. Click **Edit** button
3. Modify the information
4. Click **Save**

## Deleting a Brand

1. Find the brand in the list
2. Click **Delete** button
3. Confirm deletion

> **Note:** Deleting a brand does not delete parts. Parts will become unbranded.

## Using Brands

### Filtering Parts

On the Parts list page, use the brand filter to show only parts from a specific brand.

### Assigning Brands to Parts

When creating or editing a part, select the brand from the dropdown.
