# Glossary

Common terms used in RepairCore.

## A

**Active** - Status indicating an item is enabled and available for use.

**Admin** - Administrator user with elevated permissions.

**Assignment** - Linking a technician to a repair order.

## B

**Backorder** - Parts added to an order when stock is insufficient. The system tracks the quantity needed.

**Bulk Action** - Performing an action on multiple items at once (e.g., delete selected).

## C

**Category** - A grouping for organizing parts or services.

**Customer** - A person who brings devices for repair.

## D

**Dashboard** - The main overview page showing key metrics and quick actions.

**Device** - A customer's item brought in for repair (phone, laptop, etc.).

**Draft** - Initial status for new items not yet published or active.

## I

**Inactive** - Status indicating an item is disabled and not available for use.

**Invoice** - A document detailing charges for a repair order.

## L

**Label** - A printable tag for identifying devices or orders.

**Low Stock** - When part quantity falls to or below the threshold.

## O

**Order Code** - Unique identifier for a repair order (e.g., RO-20241211-0001).

## P

**Part** - A component used in repairs (screens, batteries, etc.).

**Payment** - Money received from a customer for services.

**Permission** - A specific action a user is allowed to perform.

**Progress Log** - A record of work done on a repair order.

## R

**Repair Order** - A record of a device repair job including customer, device, parts, services, and payments.

**Role** - A set of permissions assigned to users (e.g., Admin, Technician).

## S

**Service** - Labor or work performed (e.g., screen replacement, diagnostic).

**SKU** - Stock Keeping Unit, a unique code for identifying parts.

**Status** - The current state of an order (e.g., Pending, In Progress, Completed).

**Stock** - The quantity of a part available in inventory.

## T

**Tax Class** - A configuration for calculating tax on items.

**Technician** - Staff member who performs repairs.

**Template** - A pre-defined format for notifications or documents.

**Threshold** - The stock level that triggers low stock alerts.

## U

**User** - A staff member with access to the admin panel.

## W

**Warranty** - A guarantee period for repairs.

**Widget** - A component on the dashboard showing specific information.
