# Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about using RepairCore.

## General

### What is RepairCore?

RepairCore is a repair shop management system that helps you manage repair orders, customers, inventory, and more.

### What browsers are supported?

RepairCore works best with modern browsers:
- Google Chrome (recommended)
- Mozilla Firefox
- Microsoft Edge
- Safari

### Can I use RepairCore on mobile?

Yes, RepairCore is responsive and works on tablets and smartphones. However, for the best experience, we recommend using a desktop or laptop.

## Orders

### How do I create a repair order?

1. Go to **Admin > Repair Orders**
2. Click **Create Order**
3. Select customer and device
4. Fill in problem description
5. Add parts and services
6. Save

### Can I edit a completed order?

Yes, you can edit completed orders if you have the appropriate permissions. However, be careful as this may affect reports and history.

### How do I print an invoice?

1. Open the repair order
2. Click **Invoice** button
3. PDF opens in new tab
4. Print or download

## Customers

### How do I add a new customer?

1. Go to **Admin > Customers**
2. Click **Create Customer**
3. Fill in customer details
4. Save

Or create inline when creating an order.

### Can customers track their orders?

Yes, customers can use the tracking page with their order code to see the status of their repair.

## Inventory

### How does stock tracking work?

Stock is automatically deducted when parts are added to orders and returned when parts are removed or orders are cancelled.

### What is backorder?

Backorder allows you to add parts to orders even when stock is insufficient. The system tracks the backorder quantity.

### How do I adjust stock manually?

1. Open the part details
2. Click **Adjust Stock**
3. Enter quantity (+ to add, - to subtract)
4. Enter reason
5. Save

## Payments

### Can I record partial payments?

Yes, you can record multiple partial payments for an order. The system tracks the balance.

### What payment methods are supported?

You can configure any payment methods you accept (cash, card, bank transfer, etc.). These are just labels for recording purposes.

## Users

### How do I add a new staff member?

1. Go to **Admin > Users**
2. Click **Create User**
3. Fill in details and select role
4. Save

### What's the difference between roles?

- **Super Admin** - Full access to everything
- **Admin** - Administrative access
- **Technician** - Repair and order management
- **Receptionist** - Customer intake and basic orders

### How do I reset a user's password?

1. Edit the user
2. Enter new password
3. Save

Or the user can use "Forgot Password" on the login page.

## Settings

### Where do I configure my company information?

Go to **Settings > Company** to set your company name, address, logo, and other details.

### How do I set up email notifications?

1. Go to **Settings > Email**
2. Configure SMTP settings
3. Test the connection
4. Customize templates in **Notification Templates**

### How do I change the currency?

Go to **Settings > Finance** to configure currency code, symbol, and format.

## Technical

### How do I back up my data?

Contact your hosting provider or system administrator for database backups.

### Can I import data from another system?

Data import functionality depends on your setup. Contact support for assistance with data migration.
